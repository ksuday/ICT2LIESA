About Liesa

Leisa (Bach.Int Des) is an Artist, Illustrator, Designer and Educator. With over 20 years experience, her background includes Illustration and Architectural Illustration, Interior Design, Life Drawing teaching, Silk Painting, Fabric Design and Mosaic making. Leisa combines her passion for Art, it's processes and people to provide quality workshops for all ages and nervous levels within a nurturing Environment. Available for commissions via email at Contact Me.
